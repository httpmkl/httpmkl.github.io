Paint Strokes

 
   
 

Artist Statement

 

This piece imitates the process of visual artists using traditional mediums, such as paint, and creating what is the product of many individual strokes. However, the computer’s strokes are unique from humans; they’re sporadic, with varying rotations and positions on the canvas. While the piece may look aesthetically unpleasing during its chaotic development, with some time, we get to see how it slowly ties together into a unified and complete work. Although the beauty of this piece takes a bit of time before revealing itself, it goes to show how the concept of controlled randomness in generative art adds a unique layer to each piece that simply cannot be replicated by humans.
