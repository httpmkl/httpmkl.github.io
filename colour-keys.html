Colour Keys

 
   
 

Artist Statement

 

One element of digital and generative art that is distinct from other forms is the ability to incorporate the observer in its creation. Colour Keys is a piece that portrays precisely that, but to greater degrees than before; its artistic value lies entirely in the hands of the observer and what they decide to create with it. While this piece is mainly rudimentary, it effectively depicts how creative coding can allow for the involvement of multiple artists; the coder, computer, and observers interacting with and modifying the piece. This quality makes the artistic process genuinely stand out from typical forms, and the works more dynamic than one can typically expect.
